Football

Football is a sport that has been around since 1869. It stemmed from soccer and rugby, and has since been a sport that people love. Some may wonder why football is not a worldwide sport, like most others. Watch this video to find out!

What are the aspects of football?

  1. Defense
    • Tackle the other team behind the line of scrimmage
    • Intercept their touchdowns
    • Strategically stop them from progressing down the field
  2. Offense
    • Move the ball down the field with intricate plays
    • Create distractions to confuse the defense
    • Score a touchdown
    • Earn the most points
  3. Special Teams
    • Return punts
    • score extra points after touchdowns
